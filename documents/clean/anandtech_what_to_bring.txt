Depends where you are going. We lucked out in that we have a full size fridge and a microwave provided already. One brought a toaster oven. All have seen extensive use. We barely ever use the TV (had to supply it). Although the apartment is cleaned once a week, we decided buying a mop/broom would be a good idea. Garbage bags/cans.
